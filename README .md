## 📌 Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course, where I assumed the role of a Student Digital Ambassador. The objective was to build a comprehensive digital literacy portfolio that helps students understand how to navigate the digital world responsibly and effectively.

The project covers five major domains:
- Digital awareness and communication  
- Professional online presence  
- Coding and collaboration platforms  
- Email etiquette and digital communication  
- Cybercrime awareness and prevention
